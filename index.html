250 years of economic track record is not a guarentte for success, but can you afford to bet against it?

6Wave was created through the desire to see what others don’t. It was founded on a love for economic history the last five, +/-50 year technology cycles, and hundreds of smaller deb/business cycles. 6Wave was founded by Eugene Xata in 2019,  following the publication of his book. History repeats itself because human nature does not change.

Today we live in the Kondratieff Winter phase of the last mega cycle. The phase where slowing productivity of microchip is no longer able to generate the historical rates of productivity growth. Overcapacity has become a problem with  broadly declining profit margins — overleveraging was already evident last several years prior to COVID impact.

Bankruptcies and economic turbulences, structural unemployment, protectionism, civil unrests, political instability and wars are all becoming part of the new world which capital pools need to acknowledge.

Historically there are only a few big picture outcomes. Unlikely scenarios  are wars or productivity breakthroughs and more likely is Inflation or Deflation. @6Wave we believe that Fiat and Bonds have turned into vehicles for wealth confiscation and are no longer able to be the cornerstone for long term wealth preservation during the transition phase and paradigm shift. Negative yields, MMT thinking are all warning signs. @6Wave we help clients to re-position their portfolios to improve their chance of survival.
